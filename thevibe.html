Who We Are

THE VIBE STORY

The Vibe Boxing Club was born from a simple belief: fighting arts transform lives. Nestled in Kathmandu, we built a space where beginners find confidence and fighters sharpen their edge.

Our gym isn't just a place to train — it's a community. From your very first session to stepping on the podium, every member gets the coaching, support, and fire they need.

  • Discipline Over Motivation
  • Technique Before Power
  • Community First, Always
  • No Ego on the Mat
The Team

YOUR COACHES

Boxing · Muay Thai · Kickboxing

Ezekiel Pariyar

Head Coach — Boxing, Muay Thai & Kickboxing

With over 12 years of experience in combat sports, Ezekiel Pariyar brings professional-level boxing, Muay Thai, and kickboxing training to The Vibe Boxing Club. Certified in Thailand and experienced in Nepal National competitions, he focuses on discipline, technique, and real fight conditioning. His coaching style is built to help both beginners and advanced fighters grow with confidence and proper fundamentals.

Boxing · Strength & Conditioning

Sangita Rana Pariyar

Boxing Coach & Strength and Conditioning Specialist

Sangita Rana Pariyar combines boxing expertise with fitness, nutrition, and athletic conditioning to help members train smarter and perform better. With 7 years of experience in boxing, she specialises in improving endurance, strength, recovery, and overall fight readiness. Her approach focuses on building both physical performance and long-term healthy training habits.

Boxing · Fitness

Punam Pariyar

Boxing & Fitness Coach

Punam Pariyar focuses on helping beginners build a strong foundation in boxing and fitness training. With 4 years of experience, she guides members through proper stance, form correction, basic combinations, stretching, and movement drills. Her supportive coaching style helps new trainees develop confidence, discipline, and correct technique from the start.
